Food Bootique is a JavaScript-based application that simplifies the process of buying groceries online. With features like product search, shopping cart, and product filters, Food Bootique offers a seamless shopping experience for users looking to purchase food items.

### Features:

Product Search: Quickly find the items you need by searching through a wide range of food products available in the store.

Shopping Cart: Add products to your cart and manage your shopping list conveniently before proceeding to checkout.

Product Filters: Filter products based on various criteria such as category, price range, dietary preferences, etc., to narrow down your search and find exactly what you're looking for.



### How to Use:

Installation:

Clone the repository to your local machine. Navigate to the project directory. Run npm install to install dependencies. Development:

Run npm start to start the development server. Open your browser and navigate to http://localhost:3000 to view the app. Usage:

Sign up or log in to your account. Add your expenses and income using the provided forms. View your financial statistics and insights on the dashboard. Set budgets and monitor your spending to achieve your financial goals. Deployment:

Customize the project according to your needs. Deploy the application to your preferred hosting platform.
